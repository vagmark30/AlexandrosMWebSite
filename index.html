5 HEARTS HOTEL

The furnished apartments and studios "ALEXANDROS M" are located in the coastal village MALEME. Our hospitable and always friendly attitude in combination with the elegant and functional design of the apartments, make the place ideal for amazing and relaxing vacations. MALEME is a tourist developed coastal village and abstains 17 km from Chania. It is an ideal destination for everyone who desires calmness, relaxing and quietness but at the same time intense nightlife, as the cosmopolitan PLATANIAS is just 4 km away.

The ALEXANDROS M studios & apartments offers 8 studios for 2 persons, 8 apartments for 4 persons and a suite for 6 people.All have kitchenet,shower,air condition and television.ALEXANROS M also has a swimming pool,children's pool,football field, beach volley and a bar open all day, with snacks and drinks until late at night,At the bar there is satelite TV , dart board and free WI-FI internet in all common areas.In addition, a client library and a large private parking.The reception is open from 8 in the morning until 12 at midnight. you can also reach us all day and night in the phone number.00302821062653 & 00306944188800. In our Bar you can find breakfast served for 4 hours from 08:00 to 12:00 every day. You can also be served breakfast in the room after request.
